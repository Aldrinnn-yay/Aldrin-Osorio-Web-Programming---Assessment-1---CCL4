Ingredients:

  • Bread🍞
  • Cheese🧀(for better results use 3 different types of cheese)
  • Butter🧈
  • Mayonnaise (there is no emoji for mayo:()

Steps:

  1. Get your bread 🍞, and open the bag and pull out however much slices youre craving
  2. Slather them up with mayonaise on one side (not to much, not too less, but just enough)
  3. In a hot pan, melt butter and put your bread down so that the mayonaise is the first one to get toasted
    • NOTE: If you like your bread toasted on both sides then toast them on both sides
  4. Then you add your cheese(hopefully you take my advice and added three cheeses 🧀), on one bread and then add a splash of water and cover the pan to melt the cheese quicker
  5. Then once the cheese melts put the other toast of bread on top of the bread with cheese on it and...
  6. Boom! You got yourself a cheese sandwich! You may repeat until you have as much as you are craving for:D
